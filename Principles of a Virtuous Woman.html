Principles of a Virtuous Woman

  • 1. Fears the Lord – “The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom.” (Proverbs 9:10)
  • 2. Trusts in Her Husband – “The heart of her husband doth safely trust in her.” (Proverbs 31:11)
  • 3. Her Worth is Far Above Rubies – “Who can find a virtuous woman? for her price is far above rubies.” (Proverbs 31:10)
  • 4. Speaks with Wisdom and Kindness – “She openeth her mouth with wisdom; and in her tongue is the law of kindness.” (Proverbs 31:26)
  • 5. Diligent and Hardworking – “She seeketh wool, and flax, and worketh willingly with her hands.” (Proverbs 31:13)
  • 6. Provides for Her Household – “She riseth also while it is yet night, and giveth meat to her household.” (Proverbs 31:15)
  • 7. Helps the Poor and Needy – “She stretcheth out her hand to the poor; yea, she reacheth forth her hands to the needy.” (Proverbs 31:20)
  • 8. Is Modest and Respectful – “Strength and honour are her clothing; and she shall rejoice in time to come.” (Proverbs 31:25)
  • 9. Dresses with Modesty – “In like manner also, that women adorn themselves in modest apparel.” (1 Timothy 2:9)
  • 10. Is a Woman of Purity – “I made a covenant with mine eyes; why then should I think upon a maid?” (Job 31:1)
  • 11. Strong and Courageous – “She girdeth her loins with strength, and strengtheneth her arms.” (Proverbs 31:17)
  • 12. Is a Good Steward of Her Resources – “She considereth a field, and buyeth it: with the fruit of her hands she planteth a vineyard.” (Proverbs 31:16)
  • 13. Takes Care of Her Family – “She looketh well to the ways of her household, and eateth not the bread of idleness.” (Proverbs 31:27)
  • 14. Is Patient and Gentle – “The servant of the Lord must not strive; but be gentle unto all men.” (2 Timothy 2:24)
  • 15. Practices Self-Control – “She is not afraid of the snow for her household: for all her household are clothed with scarlet.” (Proverbs 31:21)
  • 16. Teaches the Younger Women – “That they may teach the young women to be sober, to love their husbands, to love their children.” (Titus 2:4)
  • 17. Builds Her Home – “The wise woman buildeth her house: but the foolish plucketh it down with her hands.” (Proverbs 14:1)
  • 18. Is a Blessing to Her Family – “Her children arise up, and call her blessed; her husband also, and he praiseth her.” (Proverbs 31:28)
  • 19. Lives with Integrity – “The just man walketh in his integrity: his children are blessed after him.” (Proverbs 20:7)
  • 20. Does Not Worry About the Future – “She shall rejoice in time to come.” (Proverbs 31:25)
  • 21. Knows How to Encourage Others – “She girdeth her loins with strength, and strengtheneth her arms.” (Proverbs 31:17)
  • 22. Is a Woman of Faith – “She is not afraid of the snow for her household.” (Proverbs 31:21)
  • 23. Seeks Wisdom – “She seeketh wool, and flax, and worketh willingly with her hands.” (Proverbs 31:13)
  • 24. Praises Her Husband – “Her husband is known in the gates, when he sitteth among the elders of the land.” (Proverbs 31:23)
  • 25. Is Faithful and Loyal – “She will do him good and not evil all the days of her life.” (Proverbs 31:12)
  • 26. Encourages Her Husband – “Her husband also, and he praiseth her.” (Proverbs 31:28)
  • 27. Has a Heart of Compassion – “She stretcheth out her hand to the poor; yea, she reacheth forth her hands to the needy.” (Proverbs 31:20)
  • 28. Practices Hospitality – “Use hospitality one to another without grudging.” (1 Peter 4:9)
  • 29. Avoids Laziness – “She layeth her hands to the spindle, and her hands hold the distaff.” (Proverbs 31:19)
  • 30. Enjoys the Fruit of Her Labor – “Her fruit is profitable.” (Proverbs 31:31)
  • 31. Trusts in God's Provision – “She is not afraid of the snow for her household: for all her household are clothed with scarlet.” (Proverbs 31:21)
